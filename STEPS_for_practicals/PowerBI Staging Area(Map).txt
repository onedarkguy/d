Steps:

1. In Power BI, click on **File**.  
2. Go to **Options and Settings** and click on **Options**.  
3. Check **Map and Filled Map Visuals**.  
4. Click on **OK**.  
5. Open a table.  
6. Right-click on any column and select **Edit Query**.  
7. Another window will be opened.  
8. The tables will now be loaded.  
9. Select the **Customers** table.  
10. Select any column and right-click to select **Edit Query**.  
11. A new window will open.  
12. Remove duplicate entries by right-clicking on the column and selecting **Remove Duplicates**.
