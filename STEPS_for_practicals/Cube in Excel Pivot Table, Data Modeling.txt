Steps:

1. **Open Microsoft Excel** containing the **Pivot Table**.  
2. Click anywhere inside the **Pivot Table** to activate the **PivotTable Analyze** tab.  
3. Go to the **PivotTable Analyze** tab in the toolbar.  
4. Click on **PivotChart**.  
5. In the **Insert Chart** window, select **Clustered Column Chart** (or any other preferred chart type).  
6. Click **OK**.  
7. The **Clustered Column Chart** will be displayed based on your Pivot Table data.
