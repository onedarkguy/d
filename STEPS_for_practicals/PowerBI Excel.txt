Steps:

1. Firstly make an excel sheet with your data
2. Open Microsoft Power BI
3. Select Excel Workbook under the Get Data option. Click on Connect.
4. Browse your file and select it.
5. Select the sheet and click on Load once the table is displayed.
6. Wait for the loading to complete.
7. The data will be shown on the screen once the loading is complete.
8. Click on the Charts icon to display the graphical representation of the data.
