Steps:  

1. **Open Microsoft Excel**.  
2. Go to the **Data** tab.  
3. Click on **Get Data** → **From Other Sources** → **From Analysis Services**.  
4. Enter your **Server Name** and click **Next**.  
5. Select any available **table** from the list and click **Next**.  
6. Click on **Finish** to complete the import process.  
7. In the **Import Data** window, select **PivotTable Report**.  
8. Click on **OK** to insert the **Pivot Table**.  
9. In the **PivotTable Fields** pane, select the required **Row Labels** for your Pivot Table.  
10. Drag the **required fields** into the **Rows**, **Columns**, and **Values** sections as needed.
